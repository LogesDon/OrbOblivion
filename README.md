# OblivionOrb

> Your inbox's black hole escape pod

OblivionOrb reimagines notification overload as a dynamic, physics-based "mental solar system" to combat decision fatigue. By visualizing obligations as orbiting orbs, it turns chaotic inboxes into an intuitive, gamified dashboard that prioritizes action while enforcing mental breaks.

## Core Concept

OblivionOrb scans emails, Slack, WhatsApp, and calendar invites to spawn orbs representing pending responses or tasks:

- **Size** reflects urgency (boss emails balloon massively)
- **Color** denotes sender relationship (red for high-stakes colleagues, green for peers)
- **Orbit speed** signals deadlines

Orbs "collide" with dramatic animations if ignored too long, triggering haptic alerts. This draws from astrophysics simulations for a mesmerizing, low-stress UX that makes triaging feel like planetary management rather than drudgery.

## Key Features

### Orb Physics Engine
Orbs gravitate toward a central "you" avatar, pulled by factors like sender influence or thread length. Responding to one scatters dependents, creating satisfying chain reactions.

### AI Response Cloning
Analyzes your past replies to generate 90% accurate drafts in your voice/tone, with one-tap approval—perfect for quick follow-ups without losing authenticity.

### Oblivion Bubbles
AI detects burnout via response delays and patterns, then inflates protective "bubbles" that mute low-priority orbs for set periods, synced to your schedule.

### Energy Debt Tracker
Quantifies ignored messages as "debt points" with visualizations (e.g., a depleting shield), auto-escalating to voice nudges or delegation prompts.

### Cross-Platform Orbit
Web/mobile sync with AR mode for desk overlays, integrating with Google Workspace and Microsoft Teams.

## Key Innovations

- **Visual physics sim**: Orbs bounce based on urgency and relationships
- **Predictive cloning**: Learns your tone for 90% auto-responses you approve
- **Burnout shield**: Caps daily orbs, suggesting human handoffs

## The Problem It Solves

Modern workers lose 23 hours weekly to notifications, burying critical feedback amid spam. OblivionOrb fixes this by psycho-profiling your habits (e.g., "You delay analytics queries on Fridays") to preemptively rank and automate responses.

## Technical Stack

- **Frontend**: React Native with Three.js for 3D physics
- **Backend**: NLP via fine-tuned Llama models for cloning/prioritization
- **Privacy**: On-device ML with optional cloud sync
